The Minds Behind

Eduarda Victoria

The Creator

Better known by the pseudonym Wahipogo or Wahi, she is the creative mind behind the universe of Profane Ties. In addition to conceiving the work, she acts as screenwriter, creative director, and is responsible for the project's administration. She also creates content on platforms such as YouTube, keeping her visual identity anonymous and never having officially shown her face.

Elisa Lana

The Artist

Elisa Lana is the second mind behind Profane Bonds. While Eduarda leads the creative and narrative aspects, Elisa is responsible for illustrating and visualizing the ideas, bringing the universe to life with her artistic talent and transforming concepts into images that enchant fans.
